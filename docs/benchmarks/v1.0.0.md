# v1.0.0 benchmarks

## Environment

- Hardware: Apple M4 (10-core)
- OS: Darwin 25.4.0 (arm64)
- Go: 1.26.3
- GOMAXPROCS: 10 (default)
- CPU governor: macOS dynamic frequency scaling (not pinned)

Measurements run from `make bench`. The reported numbers are the
median of three `-count=3` runs; allocations are `benchmem` totals.

## Notes

- The Dijkstra 1M-node measurement is from the random-graph
  benchmark (`BenchmarkDijkstra_RandomGraph`); on real road
  networks (DIMACS) the run-to-run variance is much tighter at
  similar absolute latency.
- `csr.NeighboursByID` is allocation-free *only when used as a
  direct range expression at the call site*. Storing the iterator
  or passing it across functions triggers closure escape and one
  allocation per call. See the godoc on `csr.NeighboursByID`.
